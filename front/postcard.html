Ingredient info

Name: Martini
Type: Cocktail
Alcoholic: Yes
Glass: Cocktail glass
Color: See Through
Ingredients
  • 2 1/2 ounces gin
  • 1/2 ounce dry vermouth
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon twist or olive (for garnish)
Instructions

Chill a Martini glass in the freezer. In a mixing glass with ice, combine 2 1/2 ounces of gin and 1/2 ounce of dry vermouth. Stir for 30 seconds, then strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or an olive. Enjoy!
